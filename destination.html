Regions In India

World’s seventh largest country and one of the most sought after tourist destination, India is not an ordinary holiday place but a destination that welcomes all kind of travellers to relish their kind of vacation.
This queen travel destination swanks about its diversity in landscape, cultures, traditions, and history. It’s inarguably home to several dreamy destinations with the vibe that matches yours.

North Indian Destinations

In North India, there are great opportunities for adventure in Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh, and Jammu & Kashmir where the Greater Himalayas call the brave hearts in both summer and winter. North is also an ideal destination for performing a pilgrimage as it is home to destinations like Varanasi, Haridwar, Allahabad, and many like them.

South Indian Destinations

South India comprises Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Telangana and the Union Territories of Lakshadweep, Andaman and Nicobar Islands and Puducherry. Lush green tea and coffee plantations on the slopes with a backdrop of different hues of blue sky fill your heart with solace. Not to forget the magnificent examples of Dravidian style of architectural brilliance such as the temples of Mahabalipuram, Hampi and Chola Temple among many others. South India is a treasure trove of peace, scenic views, nature’s bliss and ancient history of India’s glory. Thus, making it one of the best travel destinations in India.
